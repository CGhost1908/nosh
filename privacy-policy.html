Nosh Privacy Policy Effective Date: January 22, 2025 At Nosh, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data, especially in relation to Google Drive integration.
  • What Data We Collect Google Drive Files: We only access the files and folders you explicitly choose to interact with in Nosh. User Information: Minimal data like your Google account email is used for authentication purposes. Logs: Basic error and usage logs may be collected to improve the application.
  • How We Use Your Data Your Google Drive files are accessed only to provide the features of the app, such as syncing or file management. We do not store your files or personal data on our servers unless explicitly required for functionality. No data is shared, sold, or misused under any circumstances.
  • Your Control Over Data You can disconnect your Google Drive account from Nosh at any time via the app settings. Upon disconnection, we no longer access your files or account.
  • Security Measures All data transmissions between Nosh and Google Drive are encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Access tokens are securely stored and never shared with third parties.
