Nosh Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

At Nosh, we prioritize your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data, with a specific focus on our integration with Google Drive.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Google Drive Files: We only access the files and folders that you explicitly choose to interact with in Nosh.
  • User Information: We collect minimal information, such as your Google account email, solely for authentication purposes.
  • Logs: We may collect basic error and usage logs to help improve the application’s performance and functionality.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Your Google Drive files are accessed exclusively to provide app features, such as syncing and file management. We do not store your files or personal data on our servers unless explicitly required for functionality.
  • We do not share, sell, or misuse your data under any circumstances. Your privacy is paramount, and we take great care to protect it.

3. Your Control Over Your Data

  • You have full control over your data. At any time, you can disconnect your Google Drive account from Nosh via the app’s settings. Once disconnected, we no longer have access to your files or account.

4. Security Measures

  • All data transmissions between Nosh and Google Drive are encrypted using industry-standard protocols to ensure the highest level of security.
  • Access tokens, which are used for authentication, are securely stored and never shared with third parties.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out to us at noshthenotepad@gmail.com.
