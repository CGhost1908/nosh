Welcome To Nosh

Nosh is a powerful and simple notepad designed fordevelopers and IT students.Organize your code snippets, jot down important ideas, and keep your notes handy in one place. Whether you're coding, studying, or brainstorming, Nosh helps you stay efficient and focused.

10+ Full Language Support

Save your code snippets and access them quickly whenever you need them. Nosh makes it easy to organize and manage your code snippets.

Spotify Integration

Enhance your workflow with seamless Spotify integration. Listen to your favorite tracks, control playback, and stay in the zone-all without leaving Nosh.

Google Drive Integration

Connect your Google Drive account to Nosh and ensure your files are always safe and synced across all your devices. Experience effortless accessibility and peace of mind.

Android Support

Take Nosh with you wherever you go! Android support will bring the full power of Nosh to your mobile device, keeping your notes and ideas at your fingertips.
